    About PlexusMD

What is PlexusMD ?
plexus, n. a network of nerves or vessels in the body; an intricate network or web-like formation

PlexusMD is an online professional networking service for medical professionals and healthcare organisations. PlexusMD provides a platform for exchange of latest news, information and research in Medicine, for finding and connecting with other professionals and organisations and for discovering relevant courses and career opportunities worldwide in one place.
Who can Join PlexusMD ?
Medical professionals and students from all over the world

Healthcare organisations that work closely with Medical professionals i.e. hospitals, medical institutes, medical associations and bodies, medical journals, magazines and websites, healthcare consulting firms, pharmaceutical and medical device companies etc.
Why Should I Join PlexusMD ?
If you are a Medical professional, you can:

  • Connect with your colleagues, alumni and other professionals
  • View detailed professional profiles of other professionals
  • Stay updated of developments within your professional network
  • Learn from peers and industry leaders
  • Share interesting updates, news and information with your professional circle
  • Find and apply to interesting and relevant Career opportunities, Courses and Conferences across the world
  • Be accessible to patients worldwide

If you represent a Healthcare organisation, you can:

  • Share latest news, updates and research from your organisation with a focused audience
  • Find top talent across the world for Openings and Fellowships at your organisation
  • Publicise short and long duration courses at your organisation
  • Follow Industry leaders and reputable organisations and stay updated of latest developments

    Joining PlexusMD

I represent a healthcare organization. How do I create my account ?
In order to create an account for a healthcare organization on PlexusMD, one must be a bona fide employee of the organization and must be authorized by the competent authority within the organisation to manage the Organisation’s PlexusMD account and post on its behalf
Is it free to sign up on PlexusMD ?
Signing up and a wide range of features and services on PlexusMD are completely free for Professional as well as Organisations
I am a doctor and I also represent an organization. Do I need to create separate accounts for both ?
Yes. Professional and Organisation accounts have different features for e.g. “Post a Job opening” feature is only available in Organisation accounts while “Connecting to Professionals” is only available in Professional accounts

    Security & Privacy

Security
Security of user data is of utmost priority at PlexusMD. User data and credentials will never be shared with anyone. We have implemented 2 step encryption process for sign in authentication using the SHA-256 protocol which is considered one of the strongest security protocols. The website also uses HTTPS protocol which prevents other users sharing the same Wi-Fi network from listening to your connection. Multiple levels of technical, physical and managerial security measures and processes ensure that your data and files are protected from unauthorised access.

    Account Suspention & Deletion

How can I temporarily deactivate my account ?
You can suspend your account temporarily by clicking on Suspend account under Account settings

Posts and member information of the suspended account will become invisible to anyone on PlexusMD as well as anyone on the web – the member’s profile will not be found in search results; however member’s posts which have been further shared by other users may still persist and be seen by other users

You may reactivate and reinstate your profile as well as posts by clicking on Reactivate suspended account when you attempt sign in after account suspension
How can I permanently deactivate my account ?
You can permanently delete your account as well as your posts by clicking on Delete account under account settings.

Posts and member information of the deleted account will become invisible to anyone on PlexusMD as well as anyone on the web – the member’s profile will not be found in search results; however member’s posts which have been further shared by other users may still persist and be seen by other users.

Please remember that it is not possible to reactivate or recover deleted member’s account and associated data.
Why was my account suspended? How can I re-activate my account ?
A member account may be suspended by PlexusMD when the member is found to be in violation of any of the Company’s policies as described on the Policies page or in cases of impersonation, copyright infringement or any such serious complaint received from our users.

You can request reactivation of your account by sending an email to support@plexusmd.com
